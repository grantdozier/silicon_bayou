    The difference

    Make everything your company knows searchable.

    Most companies have years of calls, emails, quotes, and decisions sitting in storage, unqueryable. We build the layer above it: a typed object graph where a person belongs to a company, a call produces a decision, a decision creates work, and that work rolls up into an invoice, so every dollar traces back to the conversation that created it.

    • Entities are minted on first mention with full provenance, never silently collapsed into a catch-all.
    • Near-duplicates are flagged for a human, never auto-merged. We model the uncertainty instead of hiding it.
    • A single audited writer performs every mutation, and each one passes a policy gate that decides run, queue, or suppress.

    Settlement report processing: from weeks to minutes

    How custom AI-powered automation eliminated manual data entry and accelerated project delivery for a coastal engineering firm.

    The challenge

    Engineers were spending multiple weeks manually entering data from PSDDF (.pso) and SETTLE3 (.pdf) model outputs into Excel spreadsheets, then manipulating that data to generate settlement analysis graphs and account for variables like sea level rise.

    The solution

    • Processes up to 300 model output files simultaneously
    • Automatically extracts and structures settlement data with zero manual entry
    • Generates complete Excel workbooks with all required graphs and calculations
    • Accounts for sea level rise and other project-specific variables automatically

    Built with: React.js frontend, .NET backend API

    Commercial construction · Louisiana, USA
    AI + robotics

    AI-powered intelligence platform & autonomous robotics

    How a leading commercial construction firm leveraged a $30,000 pilot to gain competitive advantage through AI and robotics, adding 10 to 20 hours of productive capacity per person, per project.

    The opportunity

    Chase Group Construction, managing multiple high-value concurrent projects, saw an opportunity to gain significant competitive advantage by leveraging emerging AI and robotics technologies. Their next phase of growth required capturing institutional intelligence, increasing bid velocity, deploying autonomous site technologies, and seamless integration without disrupting proven workflows.

    The solution

    AI-powered intelligence platform

    • Intelligent quote aggregation: the team keeps texting and emailing vendor quotes exactly as they always have. The system extracts vendor details, materials, pricing, and project context, building a searchable database in the background.
    • AI-accelerated material takeoffs: upload architectural plans and the system identifies and quantifies materials, generating detailed estimates that augment the estimator's expertise rather than replace it.
    • Institutional memory: a vector database learns from every quote, estimate, and project decision, turning years of collective experience into something a new hire can query on day one.
    • Claude, with tools: job costing, RFIs, submittals, schedule, budget, and inbox search exposed to Claude through a custom MCP server, scoped by role and audited on every write.

    Autonomous robotics R&D partnership

    • Pilot site-survey robots: ongoing development and testing of autonomous robots for site surveys and building assessments, with full-scale rollout planned as the technology matures.
    • AI integration framework: the data infrastructure and models to process robot-generated data streams for predictive scheduling and proactive safety monitoring.
    • Advanced surveillance prototypes: active R&D on next-generation autonomous site monitoring for 24/7 intelligence and anomaly detection.

    Built with: React.js frontend, .NET backend API, Chroma vector database, Model Context Protocol, Microsoft Graph, QuickBooks
